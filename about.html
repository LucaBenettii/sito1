About

Graphic designer, born in 2003 in the province of Como. Since I was young, I’ve always had a strong passion for art and the creative world in general. Studying at the Politecnico di Milano gave me the opportunity to grow across different areas of design, from editorial design to branding and UX/UI. My interest in lettering has made me really focus on typography in my projects, which I consider an essential element in the creation of visual artefacts.
