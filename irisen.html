Irisen is a branding project that showcases the creation of a plant and flower business inspired by Vincent Van Gogh. The idea draws from the work and sensitivity of the famous Dutch painter, exploring his deep connection with nature as a personal refuge and a form of self-expression. This concept shapes the brand’s visual identity, aiming to give people the chance to transform their spaces into peaceful green havens, echoing the artist’s poetic vision.
