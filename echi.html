Echi del Novecento, le emozioni di un secolo is an iconographic exploration of the 20th century. Divided into six chapters, the project is built around the most significant historical events that shaped the last century. Each chapter explores key moments that left a lasting mark on history, offering a detailed analysis of their emotional impact, both on society as a whole and on individuals. The project aims to provide a compelling and thought-provoking perspective on the past, encouraging reflection on how history shapes human experience and perception over time.
