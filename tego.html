Tego is a speculative design project based on the idea that the tinfoil hat conspiracy theory is actually true. The brand positions itself within the fashion industry, offering not just protection from electromagnetic waves but also a unique form of self-expression. Tego has developed the Anti-EMF Jacket, made from Faraday fabric, a special aluminium-based material designed to block electromagnetic waves. The jacket also features a built-in detection system that displays the surrounding wave frequencies on a front screen. A standout design feature is the three inflatable spikes on the shoulders and hood, providing maximum protection for the wearer.
