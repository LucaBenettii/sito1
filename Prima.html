Prima la Libertà is a publishing project created for the Fundamentals of Design Laboratory at the Politecnico di Milano. The focus is on the historical period from the 1900s to the present day, exploring the complex socio-cultural contexts in which athletes have had to live in certain regions. We also highlight some iconic figures who have shaped culture through sport.
